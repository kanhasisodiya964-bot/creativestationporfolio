VIDEO MANAGER

Add, edit, reorder, or remove the videos shown in your portfolio section.

How to add a video:
1. Upload your reel to Cloudinary (free) — drag and drop the file in, then copy the video's URL.
2. Or paste a direct Instagram/YouTube video URL if you have one.
3. Paste that link below into "Video URL", fill in a title + category, and hit Save All Changes at the bottom.
Changes go live on your portfolio within a few seconds — no code, no redeploy needed.
